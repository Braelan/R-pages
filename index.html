Directions for this graph are in Chptr. 15 of R in a Nutshell. Here, the cost of treating heart failure is plotted against the number of cases that a given hospital takes per year. Complications and comorbidities are color coded. Costs at some facilities can quadruple the baseline for heart failure once complications and comorbidities come into play. The data set is from data.medicare.gov.
Colon adenocarcinoma tissue mean gene expession is on the y axis. Normal tissue mean gene expression is on the x axis. The line of slope 1 represents equal expression in normal and cancer tissue

Observations: a) At the green arrow, there is a cluster of genes expressing more prevalently in normal tissue. - this is likely smooth muscle tissue that was taken along with normal samples, and not with carcinoma biopsies(1) b) There are several outlier genes to look at.

The data is from http://genomics-pubs.princeton.edu/oncology/

The R script to generate the plot is my original work, available here.

Five to ten year olds faired the best, along with 30-35 year olds. Maybe these were families. Passengers in their twenties faired poorly. Were they in a part of the ship that sunk quickly? or just more selfless? The oldest age group had the worst survival rate.
